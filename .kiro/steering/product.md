# Product Overview

**Memorama Terror Mexicano** is a memory card game featuring Mexican folklore and horror legends. Players flip cards to match pairs of iconic characters from Mexican mythology and urban legends.

## Core Features
- Memory matching game with 8 pairs of cards (16 total)
- Mexican horror/folklore theme with characters like La Llorona, Chupacabras, Nahual, La Catrina
- Score tracking (time, attempts, efficiency)
- Audio effects for card flips and matches
- Character information modals with cultural stories
- Victory celebration with game statistics
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

## Cultural Focus
The game celebrates Mexican culture through:
- Traditional folklore characters and legends
- Day of the Dead (Día de Muertos) aesthetic
- Regional stories and mythology
- Educational character descriptions with cultural context

## Target Experience
- Engaging memory challenge
- Cultural education through gameplay
- Atmospheric Mexican horror theme
- Smooth animations and audio feedback
- Accessible across devices
